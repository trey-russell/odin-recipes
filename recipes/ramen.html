Budget Ramen Recipe

What is it?

The Budget Ramen Recipe is a dish designed to give you the tastiest ramen noodles for the cheapest price. It is what I personally do to make my instant ramen actually look and taste good.

What you need:

  • Instant Pot
  • Microwave
  • Stove
  • Instant Ramen of your choice
  • Meat of your choice (I use chicken)
  • 1-2 Eggs
  • Green onions
  • Broth of your choice (optional)

Directions:

Eggs

  1. Place eggs into instant pot
  2. Fill Instant Pot with 2 cups of water
  3. Set Instant Pot to High for five minuets
  4. Once the five minuets are up allow it to naturally release for another 5 min
  5. Remove eggs and chill in ice water for 2 min
  6. Peel eggs
  7. Cut eggs

Meat

  1. Season with any flavors you like (personally I use soy sauce)
  2. Cook however you see fit, make sure inside of meat is at least 165 F
  3. Cut into small pieces

Ramen

  1. Follow directions for ramen noodles on instant ramen package
  2. Replace powdered flavoring with 5 tablespoons of the broth of your choice(optional)
  3. Once Finished, add eggs, chicken, and chopped green onion to ramen bowl
  4. Wait for it to cool and enjoy :)
