Simple Fried Rice

What is it?

What you need:

  • 2 cups Jasmine rice
  • 2 cups Chicken Stock
  • 1 1/2 cups Water
  • 4 tbs butter, divided
  • 2 Large Eggs, beaten
  • 2 tbs vegetable oil
  • 3 tbs Soy Sauce
  • 1/8 tsp Salt
  • 1/8 tsp Black Pepper

Directions

  1. Place rice, chicken stock, and water in a med saucepan:bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer until tender and liquid has been absorbed ~ 15-20 min
  3. Remove heat source; stir in butter and let cool to room temp
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. add eggs and scramble, stirring often
  5. Place rice and eggs together in mixing bowl and mix together
  6. Melt remaining butter on sauce pan (medium heat) dump mixture into hot pan and add salt, soy sauce and pepper
